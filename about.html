Hi! My Name is Seb Rauschert, and I am a Postdoc bioinformatician and data scientist at the Telethon Kids Institute in Perth, Western Australia. I am passionate about data and have been working in the bioinformatics and data analytics sphere for over 5 years now. About two years ago I got into machine learning and have been working on a machine learning project involving some genetic and epigenetic data within the European LifeCycle project (HORIZON 2020).

The biggest reason, however, why I started this blog, is, that I am very passionate about coding and data analysis, but thus far I did not really have any big application ideas. I want to try this experiment now of documenting my journey from studying deep learning (using the MOOC on fast.ai) to my first own application.

So this blog will be about my work as a bioinformatician and the journey to (hopefully) my own machine learning related project.

As I really like photography and my Nikon , I will add some of the photos I take on my travels to this blog as well.

I hope I can do it with Einstein in mind:

If you can't explain it simply, you don't understand it well enough.
